4. Booking, Approval & Business Rules

The following booking rules are incorporated from the SRS and apply to all Patients and Clinic Admins:

  • Advance booking window: Patients may request an appointment up to 24 hours before the appointment start time. (SRS: Patient-1). :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}
  • Cancellation policy: Patients are allowed to cancel an upcoming appointment only if the cancellation is made at least 24 hours before the appointment start time. Cancellations violating this rule will be rejected. (SRS: Patient-2). :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}
  • Consecutive booking restriction: A Patient may not book two appointments with the same doctor within a 3-day window (no two consecutive bookings within ±3 days). (SRS: Patient-3). :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}
  • Admin schedule changes: Clinic Admins are prohibited from updating doctor schedules in ways that affect accepted or pending appointment requests. Any schedule update that would alter pending or accepted requests must be disallowed by the system. (SRS: Admin-1). :contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}

5. Appointment Workflow

When a Patient requests a time slot the system will:

  1. Verify the slot is still free;
  2. Verify the request satisfies the business rules above;
  3. Create an Appointment Request with status Pending and notify the relevant Clinic Admin and Patient; and
  4. The Clinic Admin may then approve or reject the request; once approved the appointment status becomes Approved.

Doctors can view their agenda and mark appointments as completed or update status according to permitted transitions defined by the system. :contentReference[oaicite:6]{index=6}
